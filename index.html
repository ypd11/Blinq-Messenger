Small messenger details, properly handled.

Blinq is built around everyday chat behavior: who is available, what was read, how you appear, and how you get back into your account.

Private chats

Send text, rich messages, drawings, images, and whistles with delivered and read status.

Live presence

Show avatar, status, personal message, theme color, typing state, and last seen.

Contacts

Search for users, send requests, accept contacts, remove contacts, and manage blocked users.

Sign in and accounts

Create accounts, sign in, sign out, change password, and recover access by email code.

Offline delivery

Internet messages can queue with limits, then deliver when the contact reconnects.

Notifications

Android push notifications and Windows notifications keep chats visible outside the app.

Rich messages

Send formatted text with colors, fonts, sizes, and HTML-style message display.

Images and drawings

Send images, save received images, or draw something and send it as a picture.

Two modes

Use LAN Mode on Windows nearby, or Internet Mode across Windows and Android.

Settings and reset

Theme color, behavior options, backups, restore, reset tools, about info, and help are built in.

Connection refresh

Refresh LAN or Internet connections after sleep, network changes, or temporary disconnects.

Windows groups

Windows contacts can be grouped, favorited, muted, blocked, and opened quickly.
